### COMMON QUESTIONS

#### [WHAT IS THE MINIMUM ORDER QUANTITY? (MOQ)](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5201)

Our custom hats start at 100 pieces per style.

#### [WHAT IS THE TURNAROUND TIME?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5202)

Most custom hat projects take 8–10 weeks from kickoff to delivery. This includes design setup, sampling, production, and shipping. Timing can shift based on complexity and approvals—we’ll confirm your timeline before moving forward.

#### [WHAT CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS ARE AVAILABLE?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5203)

Fully custom, built from the ground up: shape & fit, fabrics & colors, embroidery, patches, woven labels, interior labels, trims, closures, and finishes. If you can imagine it, we can make it.

#### [CAN I SPLIT DESIGNS ACROSS A HAT ORDER?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5204)

We don’t split a single run across multiple designs. Each design is quoted as its own style. Colorway splits are available on 100-piece orders for +$1.50 per hat.

#### [CAN I SEE A SAMPLE BEFORE PRODUCTION?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5205)

Yes – samples are available once a full order is confirmed. We’ll share photos of the sample for approval. If you want a physical sample shipped to you, you cover $40 shipping. We don’t produce samples for unconfirmed projects.

#### [HOW LONG DO SAMPLES TAKE?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5206)

Standard builds take 2 weeks. Complex builds may take 3 weeks. Add 1 week if shipping a physical sample. Approval time and change requests may extend the timeline.

#### [HOW IS PRICING DETERMINED?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5207)

Pricing is based on quantity, materials, construction, and decoration. You’ll receive a clear, all-in quote before we begin.

#### [WHAT'S INCLUDED IN THE UNIT PRICE?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5208)

Your unit price typically includes production, standard customization, and domestic shipping. Extras (if requested) may include rush timelines, specialty materials, or physical sample shipping. All will be confirmed before ordering.

#### [WHAT ARE THE PAYMENT TERMS?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-5209)

Full payment is required upfront to start production. This locks materials, confirms scheduling, and keeps your project moving without delays.

#### [DO YOU OFFER RUSH PRODUCTION?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52010)

Sometimes – rush availability depends on the style, factory capacity, and timeline. If you’re on a deadline, tell us early and we’ll review options and pricing.

#### [WHAT HAPPENS IF THERE IS A DEFECT IN MY ORDER?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52011)

If something arrives with a manufacturing issue, notify us within 3 days of delivery with photos. We’ll review quickly and work with you on the best resolution.

#### [WHAT FILE FORMATS DO YOU ACCEPT FOR ARTWORK?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52012)

Preferred formats are vector files: AI, EPS, or PDF. If you’re unsure, send what you have and we’ll guide you.

#### [WHAT IS YOUR ARTWORK APPROVAL POLICY?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52013)

All artwork and mockups must be approved in writing before production begins. Please review sizing, placement, spelling, and colors carefully. Final output is based on what’s approved.

#### [CAN I MAKE CHANGES AFTER PRODUCTION STARTS?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52014)

Once production begins, changes can’t be made. Please confirm all details before final approval.

#### [CAN I CANCEL OR RETURN A CUSTOM ORDER?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52015)

Because everything is made-to-order, custom orders can’t be canceled or returned once production begins. If there’s a manufacturing issue, we’ll make it right.

#### [I NEED HATS FASTER THAN 8-10 WEEKS – WHAT SHOULD I DO?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52016)

We also offer a separate blank hat program (stock styles) for quicker turnarounds. Email us and we’ll send options and pricing.

#### [DO YOU OFFER EMBROIDERED OR PATCH HATS ON BLANKS?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52017)

Yes – this is a separate program from fully custom hats.

Typical timelines: 2 weeks for embroidery/print, 5 weeks for patches/labels. Limited to available blank styles, colors, and fits. Quoted separately – email us for details.

#### [DO YOU HELP WITH DESIGN?](https://orangegoods.co/goods/hats/\#collapse-52018)

Yes – our in-house design team can help create or refine your hat design and get it production-ready.
