### COMMON QUESTIONS

#### [WHAT IS THE MINIMUM ORDER QUANTITY? (MOQ)](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2131)

Our minimum order requirement is typically 100-144 pieces per color, style, or decoration. Sizes can be split within the 144-piece minimum.

#### [CAN I SPLIT UP ORDER MINIMUMS INTO DIFFERENT SETS OF COLORS/STYLES/DECORATIONS?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2132)

Unfortunately, not. Each minimum applies to just one color, style, or decoration.

#### [CAN I ORDER FULLY CUSTOM PRODUCTS?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2133)

Yes! We specialize in custom-made goods, allowing you to fully personalize products. From colors to materials, we can help create unique items that reflect your brand's personality. [GET A QUOTE](https://orangegoods.co/contact/)

#### [HOW IS PRICING DETERMINED?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2134)

Pricing depends on several factors such as product type, quantity, design complexity, decoration methods, and leadtime. We’ll provide a clear, detailed quote once we have all the info about your order. The higher the quantity, the better the price per unit.

#### [WHAT ARE YOUR LEAD TIMES FOR CUSTOM ORDERS?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2135)

Lead times differ depending on the product. For specific details about a product, feel free to inquire, and we can provide you with an estimate. Generally, wholesale embroidered or screen printed products take about 2-3 weeks, while fully custom products may require anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the complexity. We can usually offer rush options, so let us know when you need your order and we'll work with you to receive on time!

#### [WHAT SUSTAINABLE GOODS DO YOU OFFER?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2136)

We offer a variety of sustainable options in all product categories. Please [GET IN TOUCH](https://orangegoods.co/contact/) for more details.

#### [CAN I GET A QUOTE FOR SCREEN PRINTED OR EMBROIDERED GOODS?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2137)

Absolutely! [GET A QUOTE](https://orangegoods.co/contact/)

#### [DO YOU OFFER WAREHOUSING & FULFILLMENT?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2138)

We do! Let us know your scope of work and we can get you a quote and chat through the process.

#### [HOW LONG DO CUSTOM DESIGNS TAKE?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-2139)

We are able to bust out designs super quickly, usually within 1-2 weeks. For the larger packages, we may require 3 weeks. Let us know your deadline and we can work to meet it!

#### [HOW MUCH DO CUSTOM DESIGNS COST?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-21310)

We have design packages ranging from $3,000 - $9,500. View our packages & pricing [HERE](https://orangegoods.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/OrangeGoods_DesignDeck.pdf)

#### [CAN YOU CREATE A SINGLE DESIGN?](https://orangegoods.co/\#collapse-21311)

We don't offer single designs. We provide graphics packages ranging from 5 - 30 designs. View our Design Deck [HERE](https://orangegoods.co/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/OrangeGoods_DesignDeck.pdf)
